Hi, I'm Josh

I/O Psychologist, Leadership Developer, and Passionate Advocate for Holistic Wellness

My Story

My journey into wellness and leadership began with a deep question: Why do some people thrive while others struggle, even when they have similar resources and opportunities?

As an I/O psychologist, I spent years studying human behavior, organizational dynamics, and the science of peak performance. I realized that true transformation doesn't come from quick fixes or motivational quotes—it comes from understanding how our minds work and implementing evidence-based practices.

🧖‍♂️

Bridging Science and Wellness

My background in I/O psychology taught me to look at the whole person—not just individual symptoms or behaviors, but the interconnected systems of mind, body, and environment. I've worked with leaders and teams to understand what really drives performance, resilience, and fulfillment.

As a leadership developer, I've guided countless professionals through transformation. I've learned that sustainable change requires more than mindset shifts. It requires:

  • Evidence-based practices grounded in neuroscience and psychology
  • A holistic approach that addresses mental, physical, and spiritual well-being
  • Practical strategies you can implement immediately, not theoretical concepts
  • Community and connection—we thrive when we're not alone

The Cooking Connection

But there's another crucial part of my story: my passion for cooking. I discovered early on that nourishing your body with real, whole foods is one of the most powerful longevity interventions available. Cooking is meditation. It's creativity. It's an act of self-love.

In the kitchen, I learned the same lessons as in organizational psychology: intention matters, quality ingredients matter, and the process is just as important as the outcome. A home-cooked meal prepared with presence and care is not just nutrition—it's a daily practice in wellness.

Why Blue Mind Body Soul

Blue Mind Body Soul exists because I believe lasting transformation comes at the intersection of three elements:

  • Mind: Clarity, emotional intelligence, and mental resilience through science-backed practices
  • Body: Longevity through nutrition, movement, and physical wellness rooted in research
  • Soul: Purpose, meaning, and connection to something larger than ourselves

Everything I share—from podcast episodes to recipes to resources—is grounded in my own practice and tested with people I work with. I don't promote anything I haven't used or deeply researched.

My Mission

My mission is simple: to empower you to achieve longevity and prosperity through holistic mental, physical, and spiritual well-being. I want to help you:

  • Understand the science behind wellness and longevity
  • Develop sustainable habits that actually stick
  • Connect your daily practices to a deeper sense of purpose
  • Build a life of clarity, vitality, and meaning

Because when you're thriving in mind, body, and soul, everything else becomes possible.
