Overview

Blue Mind Body Soul ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect when you visit bluemindbodyandsoul.com, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Information We Collect

Information collected automatically: Like most websites, we receive standard log data — IP address, browser type, pages visited, referring URL, and timestamps — to help us understand how the site is used and to detect abuse.

Cookies and similar technologies: We use cookies for basic functionality and to measure traffic. You can disable cookies in your browser; some site features may not work without them.
